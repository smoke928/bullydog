WHAT YOU CAN ADJUST

  • Rear O2's Disabled

    Disables the Rear O2s and eliminates CEL caused by rear O2 removal.

  • Shift Firmness Control

    Allows you to change the transmissions shift pressure. Controls how firm the shifts will feel. * Vehicles with Automatic Transmissions ONLY

  • Fuel Injectors

    Allows you to adjust for different sized fuel injectors.

  • Axle Ratio

    Allow you to set the axle ratio (gear ratio) to correct shift patterns and the speedometer on your vehicle.

  • CMCV Delete Plates

    Allows you to disable the CMCV (Charge Motion Control Valve) in your vehicles PCM to prevent a Check Engine light and other issues associated with removing the CMCV plates in your vehicle.

  • Aftermarket Intake

    Allows an aftermarket intake to be run on the vehicle without a Check Engine Light. For Intake Kits that require tuning, this will provide a selection of available intake kits.

  • Speed Limiter

    Allows you to Remove, Increase or Decrease the vehicles speed limiter.

  • Stores Stock Tune

    Stores a true copy of your vehicle's stock file before programming. Used for warranty repairs and unlocking the programmer.

  • Idle Speed

    Allows you to Increase or Decrease the vehicles idle speed.

  • Supports Custom Tunes

    Supports and holds custom tunes.

  • Traction Control

    Allows you to disable the vehicles Traction Control without using the button in the vehicle.

  • Fan Control

    Lowers the Temperature at which the Fan turns on.

  • Spark Modification by RPM

    Allows you to Add or Remove Timing by RPM Range. (0-2000)(2000-4000)(4000-8000)

  • Rev / RPM Limiter in Drive

    Allows you to Remove, Increase or Decrease the vehicles Rev / RPM limiter in Drive.

  • WOT Fuel

    Allows you to Add or Remove Fuel at Wide Open Throttle.

  • Rev / RPM Limiter Neutral

    Allows you to Remove, Increase or Decrease the vehicles Rev / RPM limiter in Neutral.

Great monitor!

SEPTEMBER 29, 2013
Russell Henning Miami

I ordered this from my local diesel specialty shop for my 03 Excursion w/6.0L and had it loaded with custom Innovative Diesel tunes and I loaded the street tune and the truck was a different vehicle. It is faster, stronger yet gets the same economy as stock. The custom tunes are better than the canned ones that it comes with. It is a great monitor with several selections that you can choose how the information is presented. I like the performance tests that incl 0-30, 0-60 and 1/4 mile time and speed. It is a convenient way to test your vehicle and any mods you make. The touch screen is a little sensitive, sometimes when you want to scroll it selects on mines but I just go back to the previous menu when this happens. To those with the infamous 6.0L Powerstroke this is invaluable because you can always see your FICM voltage, EOT and ECT which are good at prediction future problems.
